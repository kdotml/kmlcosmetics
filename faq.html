Shipping

Why is my order not available for Cash on delivery?

Can I still cancel my order?

We are sorry in that case because we only offer Cash on Delivery with following terms:

We offer Cash-on-Delivery (COD) for Metro Manila, Batangas, Bulacan, Cavite, Laguna, Rizal, Bataan, Benguet, Cagayan Valley, Isabella, La Union, Nueva Ecija, Nueva Vizcaya, Pampanga, Pangasinan, Tarlac, Zambales, Albay, Camarines Norte, Camarines Sur, Laguna, Quezon, Catanduanes, Cebu, Iloilo, Leyte, Negros Occidental, Davao, and Misamis Oriental orders, up to a maximum amount of P3,000.00 per cart. If Cash-on-Delivery is not available, you can check out other payment options.

Unfortunately, if our team already confirmed your order, you cannot cancel your order anymore, but if your order is still being processed you can contact our email to ask for a cancelation request. Please do include your order number, name, address, and date of order. Please wait for a confirmation link that we will send to ensure that your order has been canceled. The confirmation link will arrive within 24 hours, if it has been already 24 hours please contact our customer service.

Delivery

Why is my order taking too long?

Where can I track my order?

We do not have control with the delivery. Please do expect delivery with the following terms:

Metro Manila: Within 24 hours of the next business day

Nationwide: Within 5 to 7 business days (does not include Saturday and Sunday)

Refund/Return

Can I refund/return my order?

How long will it take for my order to be replaced/returned?

Your purchases can be easily returned for no charges (no return shipping or handling fees) but should be returned within one week of purchase. to any of our couriers or by mail if you use our pre-paid return shipping label We regret that we are unable to refund your original shipping charges, if applicable, unless the return was a result of our error. Please allow two weeks for your return to be processed if sent by mail. You will receive an email confirmation once your return has been processed. Your financial institution may take longer to reflect the transaction.

Replacement process begins after we have completed evaluating your returned product. This quality evaluation process may take about 2 business days. Once the item has undergone the quality evaluation process and verified the return is valid, you can expect the replacement to be delivered with in the same time we had processed the delivery of your original order before it was returned. It will also depend with the time of your request.
